Mutton Fry

Craving for a quick and easy mutton recipe, then this recipe is perfect to satiate your taste buds. Easy-to-prepare, what makes this mutton recipe a perfect treat for your taste buds is the melange of spices, stir fried to perfection. Mutton Fry is an authentic Indian recipe, which tastes best when served with Biryani, Jeera Rice,Pulao, and can also be relished with Naan, Roti, Paratha to name a few. This dry mutton recipe goes well with almost everything and can also make for a delicious appetizer. Especially, when you have a get-together or any special occasion at home. In fact, it is one of those meat recipes, which don't require much effort to prepare and can be quickly made, if all the ingredients are in place. Making this easy mutton recipe at home will give you the liberty to control the quality as well quantity of this dish. Apart from that making this meat delicacy at home simply amps up the hygiene and health factor as the ingredients used are usually of good quality. If you have fussy eaters coming over a party and you want to serve them something good, then this dish can make for a nice starter or appetizer. In fact, it is a perfect thing to pack for potlucks, picnic or road trips as it is dry and non-messy. Also, if you want to accentuate the taste of this dish, marinate the mutton pieces for a longer time, this will make the pieces absorb the spice marination.
